Media Formats

Bob Dylan has created original musical content from 1962 to 2020. Over the years,Dylan has created music spanning many different genres. Since 1962, he has released 33 original studio albums and released them on different formats.Take a look at my timemapper to learn more!

Format Visualizations

Here are my svgs that I made to show which formats Dylan released music on.

The more recent the format is,the less albums are released on them. This makes sense because as more formats were becoming popularized,Dylan shifted towards releasing his music on newer platforms.Although,Dylan continued to stay loyal to vinyl.

Dylan continued his trend of releasing fewer albums on older formats but still released all of his albums on vinyl. He also releases his albums on more platforms than in his early career.
